APP PRIVACY

App Store Privacy Policy

This policy applies to senote applications distributed via the App Store. It is separate from the website privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage data (via Firebase Analytics): screen views, interaction events, usage timing, and device/app information (OS version, app version, language, approximate region).
  • Identifiers: app instance ID and Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA, only when ATT permission is granted). IDFA can be opted out of (restricted or reset) via device settings.
  • Optional user input: some apps may request optional inputs such as age for statistical improvement (can be skipped).
  • Support inquiries: information submitted when contacting us (used only for support).

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect sensitive data such as contacts, photos, or microphone data unless required by app features.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Feature improvement, quality enhancement, and issue analysis
  • Understanding usage trends to improve UI and app behavior
  • Ad delivery, ad performance measurement, and fraud prevention
  • Responding to user inquiries

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following service:

  • Google Firebase Analytics (analytics)
  • Ad delivery services (Google AdMob)

Some apps display in-app ads. Ad delivery may use advertising identifiers (IDFA) and device information.

If in-app purchases are offered in some apps, payment processing is handled by Apple systems, and we may use purchase status information only as needed to provide paid features.

5. Age-Related Handling

Target age depends on each app's store listing. When age input is available, it is optional (skippable) and used only for stated purposes such as statistical improvement.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

In accordance with applicable laws, we will respond to deletion requests to the extent we can reasonably identify the relevant data. Some information may not be deleted immediately due to technical or legal constraints.

7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access, leakage, alteration, or loss of collected data.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy in response to feature changes or legal requirements. The latest version will be published on this page.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us through the contact page.
