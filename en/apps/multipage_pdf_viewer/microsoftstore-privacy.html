MultiPage PDF Viewer Privacy Policy (Microsoft Store Version)

This policy applies to MultiPage PDF Viewer distributed by senote via Microsoft Store.

1. Information Stored on Your Device

  • Paths of opened PDF files and recently used file history
  • App settings and display state

2. Storage Location

The above information is stored locally on your device.

3. Purpose of Use

  • To make it easier to reopen PDF files
  • To restore previous display state and maintain usability

4. External Transmission

This app does not transmit the above information to external services.

5. Contact Information

Information you provide when contacting us is used only for responding to your inquiry.

6. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy in response to feature changes or legal requirements. Updates will be published on this page.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us through the contact page.
