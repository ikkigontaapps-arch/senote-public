Privacy Policy

  • Browsing data: We plan to use Google Analytics to improve the site.
  • Advertising data: If we introduce Google AdSense, cookies and device information may be used.
  • Inquiry data: Information submitted via Google Forms (name, email address, inquiry text, etc.).

2. Purpose of use

  • Service improvement and quality enhancement

Cookies may be used for analytics and advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some functions may be limited.

4. Third-party sharing

We do not provide personal information to third parties without consent unless required by law. Inquiry data (name, email, inquiry text) is not shared with ad providers.

5. Security

We manage collected information appropriately and strive to prevent unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or leakage.
